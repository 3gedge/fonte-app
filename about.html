About Fonte

We think managing crypto
should feel calm.

Most people do not want to watch charts at 2am. They want their money working quietly in the background while they get on with their life. That is the entire reason Fonte exists.

Crypto made a promise it never kept for normal people: real yield, open markets, money that works for you. Then it buried that promise under dashboards, jargon, and a hundred ways to lose everything by 9am.

We built Fonte for the person who believes in that promise but has zero interest in becoming a full-time trader. You should be able to earn a serious return on your crypto the same way you earn one on the rest of your money, by handing the hard part to people who do this all day, and then going to live your life.

So that is what we do. We hold a 50/50 BTC & ETH basket through the post-halving bull market, then rotate into a 100% auto-rebalanced stablecoin LP (msUSD/USDC on Aerodrome) in the bear, a rules-based, halving-cycle strategy. We run it for you automatically. You open the app when you feel like it, see exactly what you own and which phase you are in, and close it again.

And we do all of it without ever holding your money. Your funds live in a smart wallet only you can withdraw from. We get a narrow, revocable permission to manage strategies, never to move your money out. If you ever want to leave, you revoke us and withdraw everything in a couple of clicks. No notice, no lock-in, no penalty. That principle is not a feature we added. It is the foundation we started from.

What we believe

A few principles we will not bend on.

These are not slogans. They are the rules that decide what we build, what we refuse to build, and how we talk to you.

Honesty over hype

We show you the realistic worst-case dip before you invest a cent, not just the rosy target. If we cannot beat simply holding Bitcoin, we will tell you. No moon promises, no fine print games.

Your keys, always

We can manage your strategies. We can never withdraw your money. That line is enforced by the wallet itself, not by our good intentions. You hold the only door out.

Yield first, speculation last

The backbone of every portfolio is the BTC & ETH basket, held only through the bull half of the cycle. When the cycle turns, we step aside into a stablecoin LP and stay in dollars. The timing is the discipline, not leverage or guesswork.

Plain language

If a sentence needs a glossary, we rewrite it. You should understand what your money is doing, what it earns, and what we charge, without a finance degree or a single acronym we have not explained.
