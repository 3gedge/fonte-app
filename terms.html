2 The service

Fonte builds and manages a crypto portfolio strategy on your behalf. We hold a basket of major assets (Bitcoin and Ethereum) during the post-halving bull phase and rotate into a stablecoin liquidity position (currently the msUSD/USDC pool on Aerodrome), automatically rebalanced to stay in range, during the bear phase, on a rules-based halving-cycle schedule, rebalancing those positions over time. The specific stablecoin venue may change if a pool is deprecated or a better one becomes available.

We manage strategies. We do not take custody. Your assets remain in a smart wallet that only you control. You grant Fonte a narrow, revocable permission to perform management actions inside vetted protocols. That permission never includes the ability to withdraw or transfer your assets to anyone but you.

The service is provided on an "as is" and "as available" basis. We do our best to keep it running and performing well, but we do not guarantee uninterrupted access, specific results, or that any strategy will be profitable.

4 Risks

Crypto is volatile and carries real risk of loss. By using Fonte you acknowledge and accept the following, among others:

  • Market risk. Crypto prices can fall sharply and quickly. The value of your portfolio can drop substantially, and a steep drawdown should be expected at some point in any cycle.
  • Total loss is possible. You can lose money, up to and including your entire investment.
  • Smart-contract risk. The protocols we use are software. Bugs, exploits, or failures, even in audited and established protocols, can result in partial or total loss of funds.
  • Liquidity and execution risk. Some positions take time to unwind, and prices may move against you during entry, exit, or rebalancing.
  • Counterparty and protocol risk. Lending, trading, and liquidity venues (e.g. Aave, Aerodrome, and stablecoin issuers such as Metronome/msUSD) can change parameters, pause, or fail in ways outside our control.
  • Stablecoin and liquidity-pool risk. During the bear phase, funds are held in a stablecoin liquidity pool. Stablecoins, particularly smaller, synthetic ones such as msUSD, can lose their peg to the US dollar. If a stablecoin in the pool depegs, the auto-rebalancing position can accumulate the depegged asset and lose value. A stablecoin LP is not a bank deposit and is not principal-protected.

Past performance, targets, and benchmarks are not guarantees. We aim to show you the realistic worst case for each portfolio, but the actual outcome may be worse.

5 Fees

Fonte charges a single performance-based fee: 10% of the yield and realized profit we earn for you. There is no management fee, no fee to join, and no fee to leave.

  • Stablecoin LP yield & cycle gains. 10% of the stablecoin LP yield earned in the bear phase (fees and rewards, accrued continuously, charged only on yield earned) and 10% of realised BTC/ETH cycle gains above a high-water mark. Never on your deposit.
  • Trading positions. 10% of realized profit when a position closes in the green, subject to a high-water mark.
  • High-water mark. If a position dips and later recovers, you pay nothing until you are back above your previous peak. You never pay twice for the same gains.
  • Flat or losing periods. If we do not earn you a profit, you pay nothing.

Network gas costs and third-party protocol fees are separate from Fonte's performance fee and are borne by your wallet as part of normal on-chain activity.

6 Non-custodial and withdrawals

You retain ownership and control of your assets at all times. Fonte's permission is limited to management actions and is revocable by you at any moment.

You can withdraw anytime. There is no lock-up and no penalty. Revoke our permission and withdraw your funds whenever you choose. Some positions may take a short period to unwind before the underlying assets are fully liquid, but nothing holds your funds hostage.

Because you hold your own keys, you are responsible for safeguarding them. We cannot recover lost keys or reverse transactions you authorize.
