Build on Fonte

Already have a dashboard? Plug it into our API.

Plenty of RIAs and family offices already run their own client portal or portfolio system. You do not have to switch. Pull every Fonte number, NAV, holdings, performance, fees, and the full audit trail, straight into your own stack with a read-only REST API, reconciled to on-chain truth, per client and book-wide.

What you can pull

  • Portfolio: live NAV, holdings, allocation, cash and cycle phase
  • Performance: returns, drawdown, since-inception, by period
  • Fees: accrued and charged, against each client's high-water mark
  • Audit: every rebalance, fee, and permission change, anchored to a tx hash
  • Webhooks: push events (rebalance, fee charged, deposit, phase change) so your dashboard updates live
  • Read-only by design: scoped keys per team, rate-limited. Keys can never move funds.
